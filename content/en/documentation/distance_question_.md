### distance?
```red
USAGE:
     DISTANCE? A B

DESCRIPTION: 
     Returns the distance between 2 points or face centers. 
     DISTANCE? is a function! value.

ARGUMENTS:
     A            [object! pair!] "First face or point."
     B            [object! pair!] "Second face or point."

RETURNS:
     Distance between them.
     [float!]

```
