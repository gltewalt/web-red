### pick

USAGE:
     PICK series index

     PICK is an action! value.

ARGUMENTS:
     series       [series! port! bitset! pair! tuple! money! date! time!] 
     index        [scalar! any-string! any-word! block! logic! time!] 

RETURNS:
     [any-type!]
